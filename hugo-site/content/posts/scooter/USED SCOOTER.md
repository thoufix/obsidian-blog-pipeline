# Honda Activa 3G (2016) – Second-Hand Inspection Checklist

A practical checklist to inspect a used **Honda Activa 3G (2016)** before buying.

---

# 1. Seller Information

Ask these questions first:

- Why are you selling the scooter?
- Are you the **first owner**?
- Do you have **service records or bills**?

Also verify:

- RC Book / Smart Card
- Insurance validity
- Chassis number and engine number match the RC

### Red Flags

- Seller avoids answering questions
- Seller says it belongs to a **friend or relative**
- Missing RC or insurance

---

# 2. Mileage Check

For a **2016 scooter**, realistic mileage should be:

| Mileage | Meaning |
|-------|--------|
| 20,000 – 45,000 km | Normal |
| Below 10,000 km | Suspicious |
| Above 60,000 km | Heavily used |

Check if wear matches mileage.

Look at:

- Footboard scratches
- Seat wear
- Brake lever wear
- Kick starter wear

If parts are worn but mileage is very low, the **odometer may have been tampered with**.

---

# 3. Cold Start Test

Some sellers **pre-start the engine** before you arrive.

When you arrive:

1. Touch the **silencer/exhaust**
2. Touch the **engine casing**

If warm, the engine was started recently.

Ask the seller to start it **in front of you when cold**.

Healthy engine:

- Starts within **1–2 self starts**
- Smooth idle
- No knocking noise

---

# 4. Accident & Body Damage

Look for signs of crashes.

Check:

- Front fork alignment
- Handlebar straightness
- Panel gaps
- Bent crash guard
- Scratches on engine cover

Minor scratches are normal.

Avoid if you see:

- Bent forks
- Misaligned panels
- Frame damage

---

# 5. Brake System

Activa 3G comes with **drum brakes (front and rear)**.

Check:

- Brake lever feel
- Stopping performance
- Brake noise

Squealing usually means **worn brake shoes**.

---

# 6. Suspension Check

Press the **front suspension**.

It should:

- Compress smoothly
- Return slowly
- Not bounce repeatedly

Inspect for:

- Oil leakage from front forks
- Weak rear suspension

Rear suspension replacement:

₹1200 – ₹2000.

---

# 7. Tire Condition

Check tire wear indicators.

Also check **manufacturing date code**.

Example:

```

2319

```

Meaning:

23rd week of 2019.

Avoid tires older than **5–6 years**.

New tire cost:

₹1200 – ₹2000 each.

---

# 8. Engine Type

Activa 3G uses a **carburetor engine**.

This is normal for this model.

Carburetor engines are fine if:

- Smooth running
- No black smoke
- Good pickup

They are also **cheap to repair**.

---

# 9. Oil Leak Check

Look under the engine.

Check for:

- Oil drops
- Wet surfaces
- Oil around crankcase
- Oil near rear wheel

Small dirt is normal.

Fresh wet oil indicates a leak.

---

# 10. CVT Transmission Test

During test ride:

Accelerate slowly.

Scooter should:

- Move smoothly
- No jerking
- No vibration

Warning signs:

- Shaking while accelerating
- Loud whining noise
- Delay before moving

Typical repair cost:

₹2000 – ₹4000.

---

# 11. Electrical System

Check:

- Headlight
- High beam
- Indicators
- Brake light
- Horn
- Self start
- Fuel gauge
- Speedometer

---

# 12. Chassis Number Inspection (Very Important)

Locate the **chassis number**.

Ensure it is:

- Clearly readable
- Not scratched
- Not repainted
- Not welded over

Tampered chassis numbers are a **major red flag**.

---

# 13. Typical Market Price

For a **2016 Honda Activa 3G** in India:

| Condition | Expected Price |
|----------|---------------|
| Good | ₹35,000 – ₹40,000 |
| Average | ₹30,000 – ₹35,000 |
| Suspiciously Cheap | Below ₹25,000 |

Very low prices may indicate hidden issues.

---

# Activa-Specific Things to Check

These are issues commonly reported specifically for the **Honda Activa 3G (2016)**.

---

## CVT Belt Condition

Replacement interval:

**25,000 – 30,000 km**

Signs of wear:

- Slow pickup
- Jerky acceleration
- Whining noise

Replacement cost:

₹1200 – ₹2500.

---

## Carburetor Vacuum Pipes

Check rubber hoses near the carburetor.

Cracked pipes can cause:

- Hard starting
- Engine dying at idle
- Poor mileage

---

## Rust Under Footboard

Inspect:

- Bottom frame
- Center stand mount
- Chassis joints

Surface rust is acceptable.

Deep rust is not.

---

## Battery Health

Symptoms of weak battery:

- Self start fails
- Dim lights
- Only kick start works

Battery replacement:

₹900 – ₹1500.

---

## Spark Plug / Idle Stability

Let scooter idle for **1 minute**.

If engine stalls or struggles, servicing is required.

---

## Seat Lock

Check:

- Seat opens smoothly
- Lock works
- Storage closes properly

Repair cost:

₹300 – ₹700.

---

## Wheel Bearing Noise

During test ride listen for:

Grinding or humming noise.

Repair cost:

₹300 – ₹600.

---

## Rear Brake Cable

Check if:

- Brake lever travels too far
- Braking feels weak

Usually a cheap repair.

---

## Fuel Tank Rust

Open fuel cap and inspect tank.

Look for:

- Rust
- Dirt
- Debris

Dirty tank can damage the carburetor.

---

# Quick Inspection Checklist

When inspecting the scooter:

- Cold start
- Engine sound
- Chassis number
- Handlebar alignment
- Tire condition
- Brake feel
- Test ride vibration
- RC and insurance
- Odometer vs wear
- Oil leaks

---

# Notes

The **Honda Activa 3G** is a very reliable scooter.

With proper maintenance it can run:

**80,000 – 100,000 km easily.**
